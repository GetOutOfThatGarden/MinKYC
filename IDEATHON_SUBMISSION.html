The Ideathon Challenge

As part of the guided ideation session at Dogpatch Labs, MinKYC is being pressure-tested to provide a concrete, buildable foundation for the upcoming Colosseum Hackathon. We are focusing on real-world privacy problems and Solana's unique advantages in local cryptographic verification.

MinKYC is the privacy-first compliance layer for Solana Mobile. We eliminate KYC data breach liability by proving identity with on-device Zero-Knowledge cryptography—designed specifically for the Solana Seeker.

In the Web3 ecosystem, users demand privacy, yet regulatory pressures force platforms to collect vast amounts of sensitive personal data. MinKYC solves this paradox by turning the smartphone into a localized compliance hub. Users verify their identity entirely on their device, sharing only mathematical proofs—never their underlying data.

The "Toxic Asset" Problem

For Users: The Identity Honeypot

Centralized KYC databases act as massive honeypots for hackers. When breached, users suffer catastrophic identity theft that cannot be undone.

For Platforms: Crippling Liability

Holding user data is a Toxic Liability. The costs of secure storage and regulatory compliance (GDPR/CCPA) stifle early-stage innovation.

The Solution: Zero-Knowledge KYC

MinKYC moves the verification process to the edge—directly onto the user's mobile device—leveraging Noir ZK circuits.

  • Local Data Extraction: NFC passport scanning happens entirely in the device enclave. PII never touches a server.
  • ZK Proof Generation: The app generates Noir ZK proofs locally (e.g., "Age > 18") with zero data egress.
  • Solana Verification: Platforms receive a true/false confirmation via a cryptographic proof verified on-chain.

Why Solana?

To replace traditional APIs, KYC verifications must be instant and cheap. Solana's high throughput and sub-dollar fees allow for sub-second ZK proof verification and the minting of immutable audit receipts, creating a transparent yet private compliance trail.
