## Executive Summary

MinKYC is a B2B infrastructure SaaS targeting the $12.4 billion digital identity verification market. Unlike typical blockchain projects dependent on token speculation, MinKYC generates revenue through direct enterprise contracts and usage-based fees from day one.

**Revenue Model:** B2B SaaS with tiered API pricing  
**Target Customers:** DeFi platforms, crypto exchanges, NFT marketplaces, fintech startups  
**Time to Revenue:** Immediate (post-MVP)  
**Projected ARR Year 1:** $180K–$360K  
**Projected ARR Year 3:** $1.5M–$3M

## 2. Revenue Streams

### Stream 1: API Usage Fees (Primary)
**Model:** Pay-per-verification with volume discounts

| Tier | Monthly Verifications | Price/Verification | Monthly Revenue |
|------|----------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Starter | 0–1,000 | $1.50 | $0–$1,500 |
| Growth | 1,001–10,000 | $1.00 | $1,001–$10,000 |
| Scale | 10,001–100,000 | $0.75 | $7,501–$75,000 |
| Enterprise | 100,000+ | Custom pricing | $75,000+ |

**Example Customer:**
- Mid-size DeFi platform: 5,000 verifications/month
- Revenue: $5,000/month = $60,000/year
- 10 similar customers = $600K ARR

### Stream 2: Enterprise Licensing
**Model:** Annual license for self-hosted deployments

| Package | Annual Fee | Features |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Basic | $25,000 | API access, standard support |
| Professional | $75,000 | SLA guarantees, priority support, custom integrations |
| Enterprise | $200,000+ | Dedicated infrastructure, white-label, compliance consulting |

**Target:** Major exchanges, neobanks, institutional platforms

### Stream 3: Compliance Consulting
**Model:** Advisory services for regulatory navigation

- **GDPR/CCPA compliance review:** $15,000–$30,000 per audit
- **Regulatory roadmap planning:** $5,000–$10,000 per engagement
- **Custom integration support:** $200–$500/hour

### Stream 4: White-Label Solutions
**Model:** Rebrandable KYC infrastructure

- **Licensing fee:** $100,000–$500,000 setup
- **Ongoing revenue:** 20% of verification fees
- **Target:** Large fintech infrastructure providers

## 3. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta Customers (Months 1–3)
**Strategy:** Free pilot program with 3–5 Solana ecosystem projects

- Offer free API credits in exchange for case studies
- Build testimonials and integration examples
- Generate "social proof" for enterprise sales

**Target:** 
- Solana-based DeFi protocols
- NFT marketplaces struggling with compliance
- Small crypto exchanges

### Phase 2: Paid Pilots (Months 4–6)
**Strategy:** Discounted pricing ($0.50/verification) to prove value

- Convert beta users to paid
- Add 5–10 new paying customers
- Focus on usage growth, not margin

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7–12)
**Strategy:** Full pricing, enterprise sales, partnerships

- Launch enterprise tier
- Partner with compliance consultancies
- Target Series A+ fintech startups
